Home Page

This is the home page of the site. The boxes with an X through them represent images. There will be a button on the hero image (directly underneath the navigation) that will allow users to navigate to another page for the Book vacation form.

About Page

This is the about the island page to tell a little bit of history and safety. The bottom text paragraph will be an accordion FAQ section.

Entertainment Page

This is the entertainment section of the site that will display the type of Activities on the island. I chose to focus on 3 of the biggest areas (the first 3 sections) around on the bottom with the 3 side-by-side images - this will be the remaining activities.

Dining Page

This is the dining page that shows the resturants available on the island. The first is essentially a blurb about the numerous times of dining options. The grid of images will be the individual restaurants with their names and then a picture of their main cuisine.

Lodging Page

This is the lodging page. The first content/blurb will provide information about the type of hotels and qualities. The 2nd blurb will have information about the island/government's promise to have clean/friendly hotels. The grid will have a display of hotel pictures with the respective hotel name.

Transportation Page

This is the transportation page. There will be a section on air transporation, ground transportation and sea transporation. The list at the bottom will be a list of ground rental companies.

Book Form Page

This is the book form page that users can click on on the home screen. This is not accessible anywhere else and will load to a completely new page.
